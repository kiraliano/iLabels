Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What we collect

We collect only what is strictly necessary to operate the licensing system:

  • License key - generated automatically after payment, stored in our database
  • Device ID - an anonymous identifier derived from your OS and username, used only to track activations (max 2 per license)
  • Order ID - provided by Plisio to link your payment to your license

We do not collect your name, email address, IP address, or any other personal information.

2. How we use it

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Issue and validate your license key
  • Enforce the 2-device activation limit
  • Reset activations if you contact support

We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose.

3. Where data is stored

License data is stored in Cloudflare KV (key-value storage) on Cloudflare's infrastructure. Cloudflare's privacy practices are described at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

4. Payment data

Payments are processed by Plisio. We do not see or store your wallet address or transaction details beyond the order ID. Plisio's privacy policy applies to the payment process.

5. Data retention

License records are kept for as long as the license is valid. You can request deletion of your license data by contacting us - this will permanently deactivate your license.

6. Cookies

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies on this website.

7. Your rights

You may request to view or delete the data associated with your license key at any time by emailing us.
