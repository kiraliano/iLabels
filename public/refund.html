Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Our policy

iLabels is a digital product. Because the plugin is delivered instantly and cannot be "returned," all sales are generally final. However, we do want you to be satisfied with your purchase.

When you can get a refund

We will issue a full refund if:

  • The plugin does not work at all on a supported version of After Effects (CC 2019 or later)
  • You contact us within 14 days of purchase
  • You describe the issue and the version of After Effects you're using

Email us at ilabelsupports@gmail.com with your license key and a short description of the problem. We'll respond within 48 hours.

When refunds are not available

  • More than 14 days have passed since purchase
  • The plugin works correctly but you changed your mind
  • Incompatibility with a third-party plugin or custom AE setup
  • Issues caused by an Adobe update released after your purchase date

Cryptocurrency payments

Refunds for crypto payments are issued in the same cryptocurrency used for the original transaction, at the exchange rate on the refund date. Blockchain transaction fees are not refundable.

Questions? ilabelsupports@gmail.com
